Who We Are

CardCraft was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make it easy for anyone to create beautiful, personalized greeting cards that truly express their feelings.

In a world of digital communication, we believe there's still magic in a physical card that someone can hold, display, and cherish. Our tools are designed to help you create cards that feel as special as handwritten ones, even if you're not artistically inclined.

We're on a mission to bring back the joy of receiving heartfelt, personalized cards in the digital age.
